About Me

Creative Professional

I'm Aymen Mohammed, a passionate Graphic Designer, Web Developer and Social Media Manager based in Addis Ababa. I create visual content that tells stories, builds brands, and connects with audiences. While also creating visually appealing and functional websites.

With expertise in graphic design, Web-development and social media management, I help businesses establish a strong visual identity and maintain an engaging online presence. From logo design to complete social media campaigns, I deliver creative solutions that drive results.

My technical skills in web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python) complement my design work, allowing me to create and implement complete digital solutions. From concept to code, I ensure designs are not just beautiful but also functional, responsive, and technically sound.
